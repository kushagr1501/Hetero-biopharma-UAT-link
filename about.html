About Us

Three Decades Of Trust. A New Frontier In Plasma.

Hetero is one among India’s leading pharmaceutical companies and the world’s largest producer of anti-retroviral drugs for the treatment of HIV/AIDS. We are one of world’s leading producers of key Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and generic formulations with presence in 140+ countries and backed by 30 years of experience in the pharmaceutical sector. We endeavour to serve patients by moulding science and technology into therapies to improve health outcomes and people's lives.

Plasma Fractionation Facility

Our Foundation Story

Hetero Biopharma, has already established itself as a leader in the manufacturing of high-quality biosimilars. To enhance the value of our existing biosimilars portfolio, Hetero has made the strategic decision to manufacture and commercialize plasma-derived products from human plasma. This initiative is driven by the significant demand for plasma products, which serve as life-saving therapeutics for various chronic and acute life-threatening conditions.

In India, there is a substantial gap between the availability of plasma products and the demand for associated therapies. So, to align with this, Hetero has decided to address the unmet needs in critical care and other therapeutic categories. Thus, Hetero Biopharma manufacturing unit was set up in Kothur, Hyderabad, India, to manufacture Plasma derived medicinal products (PDMPs).  

Engineered for Precision and Safety

Hetero Biopharma Limited, Kothur is a Greenfield Plasma Fractionation project that has been meticulously designed and constructed in accordance with current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP), World Health Organization (WHO) guidelines, the Schedule M requirements and other pertinent regulations. The facility's layout and equipment are strategically positioned, designed, constructed, adapted, and maintained to optimize operational efficiency. This design minimizes the risk of errors and facilitates effective cleaning and maintenance, thereby preventing cross-contamination. The facility accommodates all necessary functions for the manufacturing of plasma products.

Products Powered By Purpose

In the year 2025, Hetero has successfully launched its products:

  • Human Normal Immunoglobulin for intravenous administration (5% & 10%)
  • Human Albumin (5% & 20%)

These plasma products are clinically used in Critical care, Immunotherapy, Oncology, Hematology, Neurology & Pediatric segments. The successful launch of these products reaffirms Hetero's commitment to excellence in healthcare.
