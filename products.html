Our Products

The lifesaving therapy products manufactured from plasma under different categories includes Critical care and Immunotherapy 

Critical Care

Products

  • Human Albumin 5% (IP/ Ph.Eur./BP)
  • Human Albumin 20% (IP/ Ph.Eur./BP)

Indications

Indicated for restoring and maintaining circulating blood volume, based on albumin’s oncotic, colloid-osmotic properties and for treating shock, burns etc in the critical care segment. 

Immunotherapy

Products

  • Human Normal Immunoglobulin for Intravenous administration 5% (IP/ Ph.Eur./BP)
  • Human Normal Immunoglobulin for Intravenous administration 10% (IP/ Ph.Eur./BP)

Indications

Indicated for Primary immunodeficiency syndromes, Myeloma or chronic lymphocytic leukaemia, Idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura, Guillain Barre syndrome, Kawasaki disease etc.

  • Human Fibrinogen
